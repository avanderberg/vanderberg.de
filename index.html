About me

I'm Alexander van der Berg

As an experienced Software QA Engineer based in Zurich, I'm perfectly positioned to bring your website, app or API to a high standard, free from obvious and hidden defects. I guide your product through a series of systematic and thorough tests so it performs flawlessly at every release.

Software quality assurance is the one step in product development you cannot afford to skip: imagine the checkout process fails at the crucial moment.

Raised in Hamburg, I have been living in Zurich since 2022. I speak fluent German, English and Spanish and am available on-site in Switzerland and remotely.
